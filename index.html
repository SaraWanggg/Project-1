  • Our Work

    Asian Women Development Plan - International (AWDP) was launched at the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. As a women-centered international humanitarian development organization, AWDP is dedicated to eliminating violence and discrimination against Asian women, as well as protecting and empowering Asian women.

  • Goals

    Goals: Create a violence-free community for AAPI women, providing a platform for Asian women to communicate, study, share experiences, and support each other. Raise awareness and offer tips against gender violence, equipping them with tools towards better lives.
